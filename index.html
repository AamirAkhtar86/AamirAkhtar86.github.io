About

Experienced Embedded Software Engineer with a strong background in the design, development, and deployment of robust embedded systems and complex wireless communication solutions. Specializing in Embedded Software Development, Embedded Linux, and low-level device driver programming, I possess practical experience enhancing system performance on Software Defined Radios (SDR) platforms. My expertise includes familiarity with PetaLinux, Xilinx Vitis/SDK, and Vivado toolchains, enabling me to integrate and control programmable logic (PL) IPs and custom RTL logic. Proficient in C/C++, Python, and MATLAB, I am adept at translating complex requirements into high-quality, real-time software that meets stringent reliability and safety-critical standards. Committed to driving technological advancement, I excel at leading projects from concept to deployment and fostering cross-functional collaboration to achieve innovative solutions.
